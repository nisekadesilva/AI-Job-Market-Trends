The world is rapidly shifting toward Artificial Intelligence — but not all regions, industries, and roles are benefiting equally.

This dashboard was built to explore a simple question -
        “Who is winning in the global AI job market — and why?”

Using real-world job market data, I analyzed how salaries, experience, geography, and industry shape opportunities in the AI era.

What I explored

- Where are AI jobs actually concentrated?
- Which roles are paying the highest salaries?
- How does experience impact earning potential?
- Which industries are leading the AI hiring race?
- Is remote work really changing the job market?
